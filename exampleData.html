    Job Title: Co-op or Intern, M&A and Capital Markets (JR2506)
    Job Location: Toronto
    Number of Positions: 1
    Job Description:

    Our Toronto office is looking for a Co-op Student or Intern to join the Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) and Capital Markets team from September to December 2025 and own the following responsibilities:

    • Prepare pitch-books and presentation documents in connection with business development initiatives
    • Perform market, industry and company research supporting live engagements
    • Prepare lists of potential buyers and acquisition targets
    • Assist with drafting of marketing documentation (i.e. Confidential Information Memorandums)
    • Assist with various financial analysis as required including financial modelling and comparable company analyses; and
    • Participate in due diligence processes.

    How do we define success for your role?

    • You demonstrate BDO's core values through all aspects of your work: Integrity, Respect & Collaboration
    • You understand your clients' industry, challenges, and opportunities; clients describe you as positive, professional, and delivering high quality work
    • You identify, recommend, and are focused on effective service delivery to your clients
    • You share in an inclusive & engaging work environment that develops, retains & attracts talent
    • You actively participate in the adoption of digital tools and strategies to drive an innovative workplace
    • You grow your expertise through learning & professional development.
    Job Requirements:

    Your experience and education

    • You are enrolled in a post-secondary business program with an emphasis in accounting or finance
    • You have a strong interest in Mergers and Acquisitions
    • You have excellent attention to detail and ability to perform analytical work accurately, often under time pressure
    • You have developed communication skills and a strong ability to collaborate with team members
    • You have a strong aptitude for Microsoft Excel
    • You have the ability to prioritize competing priorities with varying deadlines
    • You have strong analytical and problem-solving skills in a fast-paced deals environment.
